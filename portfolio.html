Train Your Minibot is our studio's largest project. In 2017 we participated in a one week game jam where we took a concept and created a rapid prototype. The prototype had achieved a high ranking in multiple categories during the jam; enough in fact for us to invest about a year and a half turning this MVP into a full fledged game that will soon be available for purchase on the Steam platform.



Where? Planet Dysphoria -- the manifestation of gender dysphoria.
Who? A person in their twenties experiencing dysphoria.
How? First-person-POV, mystery game with point and click interactions.

Planet Dysphoria was a game we had created over a one week period as a rapid prototype. It was meant to dispaly a very specific set of functionality and all of that was achieved as well a full multiple track OST.
